Frequently asked questions

Which languages does inTelePrompter support?

The full app — every screen, menu, and guide — ships in 14 languages. Pick yours in Settings → Language. Your scripts can be written in any language.

How do I import a script?

Library → the + menu → Import File. You can import directly from the Files app in all major formats, including PDF and plain text. You can also create scripts from scratch in the editor.

What is Smart Pause and how does it work?

If you stop reading mid-script, inTelePrompter notices the silence (after about 3 seconds — adjustable in Settings) and rewinds the script to where you last spoke, so you can pick up right where you left off. It uses audio activity detection only: your voice is never recorded, transmitted, or compared against your script content, and it politely disables itself in very noisy rooms.

How do I use Hey Telly voice commands?

Enable Hey Telly in Settings, then control the prompter hands-free while performing — play, pause, faster, rewind, and more. Commands work in your app language when that language's on-device dictation is available on your device (you can enable it in iOS Settings → General → Keyboard → Dictation); otherwise English commands are used. All recognition runs entirely on your device.

My recordings — where do they go?

Videos you record save directly to your Photos library. inTelePrompter has add-only access: it can save new videos but can never read, browse, or modify your existing photos.

Does the app work offline?

Completely. There are no accounts, no servers, and nothing to sign into. Everything — your scripts, settings, and all processing — stays on your device.

How do refunds work?

Purchases are handled by Apple, so refund requests go through them: visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple Account, and choose the purchase. Apple typically responds within a day or two.

I found a bug. What should I include in my email?

Your device model, iOS version, app language, and what you were doing when it happened. Screenshots or screen recordings are gold. We take bugs seriously — most fixes ship in the next update.
