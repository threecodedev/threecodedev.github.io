inTelePrompter — Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026 · ThreeCode

The short version: inTelePrompter collects no data. None. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. Your scripts, your settings, and your voice never leave your device.

What we collect

Nothing. inTelePrompter has no servers, requires no account, and sends no information — personal or otherwise — to us or to anyone else. The app works completely offline.

Your scripts and settings

Everything you create in inTelePrompter — scripts, display preferences, themes, calibration results — is stored only on your device, using Apple's standard app storage. If you delete the app, that data is deleted with it. We never see it.

Microphone

No voice transcription, ever. inTelePrompter never uses speech recognition for anything other than Hey Telly's hands-free voice commands. We don't transcribe what you're reading, we don't send your audio to any server for recognition, and we don't keep recordings of your reading sessions.

Two optional features use the microphone, and both operate strictly on your device:

  • Hey Telly voice commands are recognized entirely on-device using closed-vocabulary recognition — Hey Telly listens only for a fixed list of short commands (like "play," "pause," "rewind"). It cannot recognize anything else. The audio is processed in real-time and discarded immediately — never stored, never transmitted.
  • Smart Pause listens only for the energy of your voice — whether sound is present or absent — never for what you're saying. No transcription, no content awareness, no recording.

Both features are optional and can be turned off in Settings at any time.

Camera and Photos

If you choose to record yourself while reading, the video is saved directly to your Photos library and nowhere else. inTelePrompter has add-only Photos access: it can save new videos, but it can never read, browse, or modify your existing photos or videos.

Files you import

When you import a script through the Files picker, the file's text is copied into your on-device script library. Import happens only when you initiate it, and the content stays on your device.

Purchases

inTelePrompter is a one-time purchase with no in-app purchases and no subscriptions. Your purchase is processed entirely by Apple; we receive no payment information about you.

Children

Because inTelePrompter collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled, we will update this policy before that version ships and note the change here. The promise above — no collection, no tracking, on-device only — is the foundation of how inTelePrompter is built.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support.threecode@gmail.com. A human reads every message.
